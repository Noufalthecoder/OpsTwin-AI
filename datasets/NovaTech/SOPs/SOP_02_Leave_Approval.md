# SOP: Leave Approval

## Purpose
Process for requesting and approving PTO.

## Scope
Applies to all NovaTech employees.

## Responsibilities
HR, IT, and respective Department Heads.

## Step-by-Step Workflow
- 1. Employee submits via portal
- 2. Manager reviews
- 3. HR logs in system

## Approvals
Requires minimum 1 manager approval.
