# SOP: Access Management

## Purpose
Granting/Revoking system access.

## Scope
Applies to all NovaTech employees.

## Responsibilities
HR, IT, and respective Department Heads.

## Step-by-Step Workflow
- 1. Ticket created
- 2. Security approves
- 3. IT applies policy

## Approvals
Requires minimum 1 manager approval.
