# SOP: Employee Onboarding

## Purpose
Standardize the onboarding process for new hires.

## Scope
Applies to all NovaTech employees.

## Responsibilities
HR, IT, and respective Department Heads.

## Step-by-Step Workflow
- 1. HR triggers request
- 2. IT provisions laptop
- 3. Access granted to systems

## Approvals
Requires minimum 1 manager approval.

*Note: Ensure timely execution to avoid delays as seen in NOV-1042*
